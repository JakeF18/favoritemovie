John Wick 2 (Rated R)

After briefly returning to the criminal underworld to avenge his puppy and retrieve his stolen car, John Wick, a master assassin and hitman, makes an attempt to live a quiet and peaceful life. However this is quickly cut short when his home is visited by Italian crime boss Santino D'Antonio, who asks him to repay a debt he owes him. Bound by the rules of The High Table, John must repay this debt by any means neccessary.
